These are deployment steps.  If you're not the developer who pushes to the
centralized Maven repo, you don't need to do anything with this file.  It's
just a crib sheet, since the process is a lot more painful than usual (due
to the fact we upload a jar for _each_ version of Solr for each "release").

============================================================================
Release version numbers take the version from Solr and append a timestamp
in the form of rYYYYMMDD (e.g., ${solr.version}-r20130829-SNAPSHOT)
============================================================================

mvn -Dsolr.version=4.4.0 clean deploy
mvn -Dsolr.version=4.4.0 release:prepare
  When asked, reset the dev version to: ${solr.version}-r20130829-SNAPSHOT
mvn -Dsolr.version=4.4.0 release:perform
